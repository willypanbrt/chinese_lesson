Scenario Name: The Office Drama
1. Yesterday at the company, the boss gave me a very crucial task.
   昨天在公司，老板派给我一个非常关键的任务。
2. He said to me: "Since you are the most awesome, you must get the work done well today."
   他对我说：“既然你是最厉害的，你今天必须把工作做好。”
3. Even if I was very tired, I still worked until 10 PM.
   即使很累，我也工作到了晚上10点。
4. Finally, I finished all my work.
   终于，我把所有的工作都做完了。
5. Unexpectedly, this morning, my computer was broken by my colleague.
   今天早上，我的电脑竟然被我的同事弄坏了。

Scenario Name: The Job Hunt
1. Not only do I want to find a new apartment, but I also need to look for a job.
   我不仅想找一个新公寓，而且还需要找工作。
2. Yesterday, I sent my resume to that international company.
   昨天，我把简历寄到那家国际公司去了。
3. Unexpectedly, the manager called me this morning to invite me to an interview.
   今天早上，经理竟然打电话邀请我去参加面试。
4. Even if I am very nervous, I still must prepare well.
   即使我很紧张，我也必须好好准备。
5. No matter what the result is, this is a great opportunity.
   不管结果是什么，这都是一个很好的机会。
