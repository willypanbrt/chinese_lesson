Scenario Name: The Office Drama
1. Yesterday at the company, the boss gave me a very crucial task.
   昨天在公司，老板派给我一个非常关键的任务。
2. He said to me: "Since you are the most awesome, you must get the work done well today."
   他对我说：“既然你是最厉害的，你今天必须把工作做好。”
3. Even if I was very tired, I still worked until 10 PM.
   即使很累，我也工作到了晚上10点。
4. Finally, I finished all my work.
   终于，我把所有的工作都做完了。
5. Unexpectedly, this morning, my computer was broken by my colleague.
   今天早上，我的电脑竟然被我的同事弄坏了。

Scenario Name: The Travel Snafu
1. Next week I am going to fly to Beijing to attend a very important meeting.
   下周我要飞到北京去参加一个非常重要的会议。
2. No matter how expensive the ticket is, I always must buy it.
   不管机票多贵，我都必须买。
3. But this morning, I unexpectedly lost my passport!
   但是今天早上，我竟然把护照弄丢了！
4. I definitely left the passport at home.
   我肯定把护照忘在家里了。
5. My plans were completely ruined by this mistake.
   我的计划被这个错误完全毁了。

Scenario Name: The Bad Roommate
1. I have already lived in this apartment for two years.
   我已经在这个公寓住了两年了。
2. My roommate always leaves dirty dishes in the living room.
   我的室友总是把脏盘子留在客厅里。
3. Yesterday, my favorite milk was completely drunk by him.
   昨天，我最喜欢的牛奶被他喝光了。
4. Since he doesn't respect me, I then decided to move out next month.
   既然他不尊重我，我就决定下个月搬出去。
5. No matter how hard it is to find a new apartment, I must try.
   不管找新公寓多难，我都要试一试。

Scenario Name: The Job Hunt
1. Not only do I want to find a new apartment, but I also need to look for a job.
   我不仅想找一个新公寓，而且还需要找工作。
2. Yesterday, I sent my resume to that international company.
   昨天，我把简历寄到那家国际公司去了。
3. Unexpectedly, the manager called me this morning to invite me to an interview.
   今天早上，经理竟然打电话邀请我去参加面试。
4. Even if I am very nervous, I still must prepare well.
   即使我很紧张，我也必须好好准备。
5. No matter what the result is, this is a great opportunity.
   不管结果是什么，这都是一个很好的机会。

Scenario Name: The Online Shopping Fail
1. Last week, because the discount was huge, I couldn't resist buying a lot of clothes online.
   上个星期，因为折扣很大，所以我忍不住在网上买了很多衣服。
2. I originally assumed the package would be delivered within three days.
   我本来以为包裹会在三天之内送到。
3. But no matter how many times I checked, the delivery status never changed.
   但是，不管我查看多少次，物流状态都一直没变。
4. Unexpectedly, the courier left my package at the wrong address.
   快递员竟然把我的包裹送错了地址。
   (Alt: 快递员竟然把我的包裹留在了错的地址。)
5. Although the shop refunded me in the end, I had already wasted a whole afternoon.
   虽然卖家最后给我退款了，但是我已经浪费了一整个下午。

Scenario Name: The Forgotten Umbrella
1. As soon as I stepped out of the company, I noticed the sky had turned completely gray.
   我刚一走出公司，就发现天空完全变灰了。
   Wǒ gāng yī zǒu chū gōngsī, jiù fāxiàn tiānkōng wánquán biàn huī le.
2. I searched through my bag, but I couldn't find my umbrella.
   我把书包翻了一遍，但是没找到雨伞。
   Wǒ bǎ shūbāo fān le yí biàn, dànshì méi zhǎodào yǔsǎn.
3. Since it was already raining, I had no choice but to run back to the office.
   既然已经下雨了，我只好跑回公司。
   Jìrán yǐjīng xià yǔ le, wǒ zhǐhǎo pǎo huí gōngsī.
4. When I got back to my desk, I discovered my colleague had taken my umbrella home by mistake.
   回到办公桌前，我发现同事误把我的雨伞带回家了。
   Huí dào bàngōngzhuō qián, wǒ fāxiàn tóngshì wù bǎ wǒ de yǔsǎn dài huí jiā le.
5. Although I was annoyed, I decided to wait until tomorrow and ask her to bring it back.
   虽然我很烦，但是我决定明天再请她把雨伞带回来。
   Suīrán wǒ hěn fán, dànshì wǒ juédìng míngtiān zài qǐng tā bǎ yǔsǎn dài huílái.
